Academic Programs

STEM (Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics)

Our STEM program focuses on developing students' skills in science and mathematics, preparing them for technical careers.

HUMSS (Humanities and Social Sciences)

The HUMSS program offers a curriculum that emphasizes critical thinking, communication, and understanding of human behavior.

HE (Home Economics)

The Home Economics program teaches students valuable skills in family management, nutrition, and personal finance.

EIM (Electrical Installation and Maintenance)

The EIM program provides hands-on training in electrical systems and maintenance, essential for careers in technology and engineering.

ABM (Accountancy, Business, and Management)

The ABM program prepares students for careers in business, finance, and management, with a strong foundation in accounting principles.
