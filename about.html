About Kiwalan National High School

Our Mission

To provide quality education that develops well-rounded individuals capable of contributing to society and adapting to a rapidly changing world.

Our Vision

A center of excellence in secondary education that fosters academic achievement, character development, and community engagement.

Our Values

  • Excellence: Striving for the highest standards in education
  • Integrity: Acting with honesty and moral principles
  • Inclusivity: Welcoming all students regardless of background
  • Innovation: Embracing modern teaching methods and technology
  • Community: Building strong relationships within our school family

Our History

Kiwalan National High School has been serving the community for years, providing quality education and preparing students for success in their chosen careers and personal lives. Our commitment to excellence continues to inspire both students and faculty.
